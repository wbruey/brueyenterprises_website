Logistics and Travel Details

We have a hotel block for guests. We are encouraging guests to stay at the DoubleTree by Hilton San Pedro-Port of Los Angeles at 2800 Via Cabrillo Marina San Pedro, California 90731. Please refer to Horton-Bruey wedding room block while booking your room.

The ceremony is early! The ceremony starts at 10am, so try to arrive no later than 9:45am to get seated ahead of Kelsey walking down the isle. After the ceremony, you're on your own for a couple hours of downtime to get lunch in San Pedro.

There will be buses to transport folks from the hotel to the ceremony and back, and then again from the hotel to the reception and back.

To get to the ceremony, there will be a buss leaving the hotel at 9:30am, boarding starts at 9:15am. It will bring you back after the ceremony.
To get to the reception, hop on the bus at the DoubleTree Hotel at 3:15pm, it will bring you back after the reception at 9:30pm.

For those traveling, after you land at LAX, we recommend you get a taxi or uber from the airport to the DoubleTree Hotel in San Pedro, from there everything is accessible by foot or wedding bus.

For locals, feel free to park near the DoubleTree Hotel and take advantage of the busess.
